How to Calculate Your FBA Profit

This Amazon FBA Profit Calculator helps e-commerce sellers quickly estimate net profit after deducting product costs, shipping, and Amazon fees. Simply input the selling price, product cost, shipping cost, and Amazon fee percentage to get your estimated profit in seconds.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Enter the selling price of your product on Amazon
  • Enter your cost to acquire the product
  • Enter the shipping cost to Amazon warehouse or directly to customer
  • Enter the Amazon fee percentage applicable to your category
  • Click "Calculate Profit" to see Amazon fee and estimated net profit

Benefits of Using This Calculator

  • Quickly determine if a product is profitable
  • Plan your pricing strategy effectively
  • Avoid surprises from Amazon fees or shipping costs
  • Free and easy-to-use online tool for all sellers
