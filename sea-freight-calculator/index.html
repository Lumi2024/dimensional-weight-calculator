What is CBM in Sea Freight?

CBM (Cubic Meter) is a standard measurement used in sea freight to calculate the volume of your cargo. Shipping companies charge based on the container space your goods occupy, not just weight. Using this CBM Calculator, you can accurately determine the space needed for your shipment and plan efficiently.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Measure the length, width, and height of your cargo in centimeters
  • Enter the dimensions into the calculator
  • Click "Calculate CBM" to get the volume in cubic meters
  • Use this information to select the right container size and optimize shipping costs
