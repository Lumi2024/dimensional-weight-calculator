How to Calculate Air Freight Chargeable Weight

This Air Freight Calculator helps logistics managers and e-commerce sellers determine the chargeable weight of packages for air shipping. Using the 6000 divisor formula, it calculates the volumetric weight and ensures you are billed correctly by airlines and freight forwarders.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Measure the length, width, and height of your package in centimeters
  • Enter the actual weight of the package
  • Click "Calculate" to see both volumetric and chargeable weight
  • Use this information to plan shipments and avoid overpaying
