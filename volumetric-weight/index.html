Volumetric Weight Calculator

What is Volumetric Weight?

Volumetric weight (or dimensional weight) is used by carriers to calculate shipping costs based on the space a package occupies rather than its actual weight. This is especially important for light but large packages, as shipping fees are often charged based on the higher of actual weight or volumetric weight.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Enter these dimensions into the calculator
  • Click "Calculate" to see the volumetric weight
  • Use this value to compare with actual weight for shipping cost purposes
