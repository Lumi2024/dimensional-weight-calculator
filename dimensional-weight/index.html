Dimensional Weight Calculator

What is Dimensional Weight?

Dimensional weight, also called volumetric weight, is a pricing method used by international carriers. It reflects the space a parcel occupies rather than just its mass.

Formula

(Length × Width × Height) ÷ 5000

Why It Matters

For e-commerce businesses, reducing box size can significantly lower shipping costs. Carriers charge the higher value between actual and volumetric weight.

How to Use the Dimensional Weight Calculator

Enter the dimensions of your package and its actual weight. The calculator will instantly show the volumetric weight and the chargeable weight your carrier will use for billing. This ensures you never overpay for shipping.
