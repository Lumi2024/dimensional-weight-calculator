FAQ / Tips

What is dimensional weight?

Dimensional weight is used by carriers to price parcels based on volume rather than actual weight. Large lightweight packages may be charged based on size.

How is dimensional weight calculated?

Length × Width × Height ÷ carrier divisor (5000 for FedEx/DHL, 6000 for UPS).

Which carriers use dimensional weight?

FedEx, DHL, UPS, and Amazon FBA often use dimensional weight pricing for large parcels.
