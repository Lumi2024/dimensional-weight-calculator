What is dimensional weight?

Dimensional weight (also called volumetric weight) is used by shipping carriers to price parcels based on volume instead of actual weight. Large but lightweight packages may be charged based on size.

How is dimensional weight calculated?

Length × Width × Height ÷ carrier divisor

Divisors vary by carrier: 5000 for FedEx/DHL, 6000 for UPS.
